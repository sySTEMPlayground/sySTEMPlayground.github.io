About us

sySTEM playground

A teaching nonprofit aimed at applicability, the theory of learning, and changing the way students think about STEM.

What makes us different

Applicability

You know why you are learning the topics we teach you. Each course is selected to not only be interesting but to serve each student a purpose in an out of the classroom.

Unique Courses

Each subject we teach is not typically taught to our target age group. But we think these topics are introduced too late to have a meaningful impact for students, so we introduce them early.

Challenging content

We teach students to use STEM as a playground: you first climb the ladder before you go down the slide: learning is fun because of the work you put into it, not despite it.
