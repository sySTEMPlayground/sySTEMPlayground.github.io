About Us

Our mission: to give every student a taste of real-world STEM topics while sparking interest and giving them the tools to face every challenge with determination and resilience.

How the system runs

There are only two types of extracurricular courses: one that supplements the school system and one that complements it. We've all heard the cliche "teaching non-profit," but most nonprofits fall under the former category; we fall under the latter. While there are benefits to only teaching material taught in schools, that kind of education has to be personalized for each student's needs, and with underfunded nonprofits, that is very hard to do. What sets us apart is that we want the students to pioneer in real-world fields, not just pass their math test. That is why our courses are unique and almost never taught in or out of school, and why we are certain we will succeed in helping your child: we don't treat the symptoms of not being curious, but the mental block itself.
